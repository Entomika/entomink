## 🐛 **What is EntomInk?**

A visual journey where **insects' uniqueness becomes imagery**. Here, we play with ideas to turn field observations, museum collections, and laboratory research into illustrations that showcase the concealed beauty of entomology.

**Perfect for:**
- Researchers needing publication-ready figures
- Museums seeking specimen portraits  
- Educators wanting life cycle visuals
- Enthusiasts commissioning personal artwork
- Bug tattoo enthusiasts
- Anyone who enjoys bugs and wants to transfer ideas to ink and paper
